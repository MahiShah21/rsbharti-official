OUR OWN BRAND

Sita Ramam Tea is our brand, created with the vision of bringing the rich taste, freshness, and authentic aroma of premium Indian tea to every household. Headquartered in Bhilwara, Rajasthan, we take pride in offering tea that delights every sip and is cherished by tea lovers across the country. With handpicked tea leaves, modern packaging, and strict quality standards, Sita Ramam Tea has become a trusted choice for families, retailers, and wholesalers in India. Our commitment to quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction has helped us grow as one of the fastest-emerging tea brands in Rajasthan. Enjoy the perfect blend of tradition and taste with every cup of Sita Ramam Tea.
