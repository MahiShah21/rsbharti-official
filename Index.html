OUR STORY

"RS Bharti – Local Roots, Global Reach"
RS Bharti is one of India’s leading distributors and exporters of FMCG, garments, electronics, and agri-commodities. We proudly distribute trusted brands like ONN, Pink, and Britannia, and also own our exclusive tea brand — Sitaraman Tea. With a presence in many countries, primarily across South Africa, we are expanding rapidly into new markets, products, and partnerships, striving to become a unicorn in global trade.

THE BRANDS WE DISTRIBUTE

WE MAKE THINGS HAPPEN

We are a trusted FMCG distributor in Bhilwara, Rajasthan, proudly associated with leading brands like Britannia, ONN, and Pynk. With a strong distribution network, we ensure the timely supply and availability of quality products across retail outlets, supermarkets, wholesalers, and modern trade channels.

OUR OWN BRAND

Sita Ramam Tea is our brand, created with the vision of bringing the rich taste, freshness, and authentic aroma of premium Indian tea to every household.
Headquartered in Bhilwara, Rajasthan, we take pride in offering tea that delights every sip and is cherished by tea lovers across the country. With handpicked tea leaves, modern packaging, and strict quality standards, Sita Ramam Tea has become a trusted choice for families, retailers, and wholesalers in India.
Our commitment to quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction has helped us grow as one of the fastest-emerging tea brands in Rajasthan. Enjoy the perfect blend of tradition and taste with every cup of Sita Ramam Tea.
