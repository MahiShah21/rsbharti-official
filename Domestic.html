Sita Ramam

Sita Ramam Tea is more than just a beverage; it's a promise of premium quality in every brew. This tea is expertly crafted for those who demand a truly strong and robust cup without compromising the authentic traditional taste that true tea lovers cherish. We meticulously source only the finest leaves, ensuring a superior base for your daily indulgence. Each sip delivers a signature deep colour, rich aroma, and powerful flavour that provides an instant boost to refresh your senses and prepare you for the day ahead. It’s the kind of tea that truly wakes you up and offers a comforting moment of deep satisfaction. The blend has been perfected over time to consistently deliver that 'kadak' (strong) experience that stands up beautifully to milk and sugar, or can be enjoyed black for maximum intensity. If you are searching for a tea that is genuine, intense, and a true classic, your search ends here. Sita Ramam Tea ensures your mornings start with a strong and memorable experience, becoming the essential ritual you look forward to every day. Choose the tea that brings both heritage and unparalleled strength to your cup.
