“Sajish Khushiyon Ki”

Sita Ramam tea is more than just a beverage — it’s a feeling, a tradition, and a connection between people. Every blend we create is inspired by India’s timeless love for chai and the moments it brings to life. From the early morning freshness to the evening calm, Sita Ramam stands beside you in every cup, adding warmth, energy, and comfort to your day. Our tea leaves are carefully selected from the finest gardens of Assam, known for their rich aroma, strong flavor, and golden color. Each leaf is handled with care, ensuring that the natural essence of purity and freshness reaches you exactly as nature intended. We take pride in crafting tea that not only awakens your senses but also celebrates the spirit of togetherness. With Sita Ramam, every sip tells a story of quality, trust, and the joy of small moments shared with loved ones. As our tagline beautifully expresses — “Sajish Khushiyon Ki” — we believe happiness is in the little things: a shared smile, a heartfelt conversation, or a steaming cup of tea that brings everyone closer. Sita Ramam — where every cup brews happiness
