Our Story

Our story began in June 2020 with a simple belief: business should be a force for good. What started with just a bike and a dream—distributing local Indian fryums and bakery products—soon grew into a mission of creating value while making a positive impact on people’s lives.
Through dedication and quality service, we quickly earned the trust of leading FMCG giants. Our partnerships with Britannia and later Hindustan Unilever Limited (HUL) were milestones that gave us the confidence to take a bold step forward—launching our very own brand, Sitaramam. This marked the beginning of our journey toward independence and innovation in the packaged FMCG industry.
In 2024, we expanded beyond borders with our first export shipment. What started small soon developed into a growing international network. By 2025–26, we proudly established exports of electronics, garments, biscuits, and agri commodities to nine countries, cementing our place as a trusted global supplier.
Today, we stand as a dynamic and evolving company with a clear vision for the future. Our goal is to expand Sitaramam products worldwide and reach 50+ countries in the coming years.
We are not just building a business—we are building a legacy of quality, trust, and service, inspiring positive change across industries and communities.
